Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Kova ("the App") is a personal fitness tracking application that integrates with WHOOP to provide recovery-based workout adjustments. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

When you connect your WHOOP account, the App accesses the following data through the WHOOP API:

  • Recovery score
  • Sleep duration and quality
  • Strain level
  • Daily step count
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)

Additionally, data you manually enter into the App includes:

  • Body weight entries
  • Workout logs (weights, reps, notes)
  • App settings and preferences

2. How We Store Your Data

All personal data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. Your workout logs, weight history, and WHOOP metrics are kept entirely on your device and are not transmitted to or stored on any external server.

WHOOP OAuth tokens are used solely to authenticate API requests and are stored locally in your browser. The Vercel backend facilitates the OAuth handshake but does not persist your tokens or personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your WHOOP recovery metrics within the App
  • Adjust workout intensity based on your recovery score and sleep quality
  • Track your weight progress and workout history
  • Generate charts and trend analysis for your personal use

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. Your data stays on your device. The only external communication is between your browser and the WHOOP API to fetch your metrics.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists in your browser's localStorage until you choose to delete it. You can clear all data at any time through the App's Settings tab using the "Clear All Data" button. You can also disconnect your WHOOP account at any time, which removes the stored authentication token.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • OAuth 2.0 is used for secure WHOOP authentication
  • Client secrets are stored as environment variables on the server, never in frontend code
  • All API communication occurs over HTTPS
  • No personal data is logged on the server

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • WHOOP — for fitness and recovery data. WHOOP's own privacy policy applies to data collected by their platform. Visit whoop.com/privacy for details.
  • Vercel — for hosting the application and serverless API functions.
  • Chart.js — loaded via CDN for rendering charts (no personal data is sent to Chart.js).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the App (via the Export Data feature)
  • Delete all stored data at any time
  • Disconnect your WHOOP account at any time
  • Use the App without connecting WHOOP

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the project's GitHub repository.
